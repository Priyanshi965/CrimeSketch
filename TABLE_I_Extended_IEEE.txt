Notes on newly added models:
• DeepFace [9] — Taigman et al. (Meta/Facebook), 2014. Proprietary deep architecture. Used here as
  a historical deep-learning landmark. Ref: [9] Y. Taigman et al., CVPR 2014.
• CosFace (ResNet50) — Wang et al., 2018. Large margin cosine loss (LMCL) on ResNet50 backbone.
  Ref: H. Wang et al., "CosFace: Large Margin Cosine Loss for Deep Face Recognition," CVPR 2018.
• ArcFace (ResNet50) — Deng et al., 2019. Additive angular margin loss on ResNet50 backbone.
  Ref: J. Deng et al., "ArcFace: Additive Angular Margin Loss for Deep Face Recognition," CVPR 2019.
• TransFace (ViT-B/16) — Dan et al., 2023. Vision Transformer backbone with patch-level augment.
  Ref: J. Dan et al., "TransFace: Calibrating Transformer Training for Face Recognition," ICCV 2023.
• MagFace (ResNet100) — Meng et al., 2021. Magnitude-aware margin loss on ResNet100 backbone.
  Ref: Q. Meng et al., "MagFace: A Universal Representation for Face Recognition," CVPR 2021.
• AdaFace (IR50) — Kim et al., 2022. Adaptive margin focusing on low-quality images (IR50).
  Ref: M. Kim et al., "AdaFace: Quality Adaptive Margin for Face Recognition," CVPR 2022.
